Press R to roll the dice
Press E to hold the current score
Press N to restart the game

How to play:

  • Roll the dice;
  • Hold the score. The game switches to the other player after each hold.
  • The first person to reach a score of 0 wins;


ATENTION! The game also switches to the other player after a random number of rolls. All score not held before the switch will be lost.
